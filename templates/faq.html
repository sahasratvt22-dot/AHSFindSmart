Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about the AHS Lost & Found system.

How do I report a found item?

Click on Report Found in the navigation menu and complete the form with a description and location where the item was found.

How do I claim a lost item?

Browse the list of found items and submit a claim request for the item you believe belongs to you. An administrator will review your request.

Who approves claim requests?

All claim requests are reviewed and approved by a school administrator to ensure items are returned to the correct owner.

Where do I pick up my item after approval?

Approved items can be picked up at the Main Office during school hours.

Is my personal information visible to others?

No. Personal information is only visible to administrators and is never shown publicly.

What if I submitted a claim by mistake?

Contact an administrator or submit a new request with corrected information.
